What is Enviro Sphere?
Enviro-Sphere is a groundbreaking platform designed to revolutionize waste management and tackle unemployment. It provides a unique solution by allowing users to upload pictures of their waste materials, which are then converted into 3D elements. These elements serve as the raw material for designers, who can visit the platform to turn them into innovative and valuable products.
AIM

The aim of Enviro-Sphere is to revolutionize waste management and address unemployment by providing a platform where waste products can be transformed into 3D elements for designers to create valuable products.

SCOPE

The scope of Enviro-Sphere is extensive and crucial in promoting sustainable resource management, reducing environmental impacts, and fostering a circular economy. It aims to revolutionize waste management and address unemployment by providing a platform where waste products can be transformed into 3D elements for designers to create valuable products.

How to Contribute?

Users can contribute to EnviroSphere by collecting and sending waste materials for recycling and providing feedback on products. Designers can create new products from recycled materials based on user ideas, promoting sustainability and creativity.
